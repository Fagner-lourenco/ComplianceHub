Description

This dataset returns information about entities related to the queried company, including individuals and legal entities associated through corporate ties, control, legal representation, or functional relationships. The return consolidates aggregated data and detailed lists of relationships, allowing for structured analysis of the company's network of connections.

When information about a subsidiary's shareholding structure is not available from the Federal Revenue Service, relationships can be built based on data from the parent company, ensuring the continuity and consistency of the returned relational structure.

For more information on business context, applications, and use cases, see the Relationships documentation.

Technical name of the dataset: relationships.
