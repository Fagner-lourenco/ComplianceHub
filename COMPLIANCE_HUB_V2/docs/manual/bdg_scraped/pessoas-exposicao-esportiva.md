Description

This dataset identifies direct and indirect links between the respondent and institutions within the sports environment, including clubs, federations, confederations, and other entities related to sports activities. The data provided allows for the analysis of institutional relationships, roles played, level of closeness of the link, and activity status, and may also include indirect associations through family or corporate relationships, when available in the sources.

For more information on business context, applications, and use cases, please refer to the Professionals documentation.

Technical name of the dataset: sports_exposure.

Interpreting the field RelationshipLevel

The field RelationshipLevel This indicates whether the identified sporting involvement is direct (by the person being consulted) or indirect (through a relationship with another person). In indirect cases, the level reflects the degree of kinship or corporate affiliation, as per the table below.

RelationshipLevel	Relationship Types	Relationship Description
DIRECT	—	The sports affiliation of the person consulted.
INDIRECT 1ST LEVEL	MOTHER, FATHER, SON, SPOUSE	First-degree kinship
INDIRECT 2ND LEVEL	BROTHER, GRANDSON, GRANDPARENT	Second-degree kinship
INDIRECT 3RD LEVEL	UNCLE, NEPHEW	Third-degree kinship
INDIRECT 4TH LEVEL	COUSIN	Fourth-level kinship
BUSINESS PARTNER	PARTNER	Corporate relationship
Interpreting the field Role

The field Role It describes the role performed by the individual within the sports organization, encompassing executive, administrative, technical, sporting, and operational positions, according to the nomenclature used by the institutions themselves.

Office	Description
ASSISTANT COACH	Assistant or analyst participating in the technical committee
COACH	Trainer
COUNCIL MEMBER	Member of the administrative board
DIRECTOR	Director of one of the sectors that make up the club.
FORMER PRESIDENT	Former president of the deliberative council
MANAGER	Coordinator, executive, manager, or department head.
PLAYER	Professional player
PRESIDENT	General president of the club or of the deliberative council
REFEREE	Referee
STAFF	Employees in general
VICE PRESIDENT	Vice President at any level
Interpreting the field IsActive

The field IsActive This indicates whether the sports affiliation is active at the time of the query. When sources do not provide explicit start or end dates for the affiliation, the status is inferred based on the date of the last record capture: affiliations updated within the last 30 days are considered active; otherwise, they are considered inactive.
