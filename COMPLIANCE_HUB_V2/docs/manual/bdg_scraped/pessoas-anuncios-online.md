Description

This dataset gathers information about advertisements published by the individual in question on online peer-to-peer buying and selling portals and marketplaces. The results include advertiser profiles inferred from public information present in the advertisements; more than one profile may be associated with the same person, depending on the different portals and contexts identified. The data only represents the individual's activity as an informal seller and does not include marketplaces aimed at formal sellers.

For more information on business context, applications, and use cases, please refer to the Digital Presence documentation.

Technical name of the dataset: online_ads.
